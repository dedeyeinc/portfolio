Technical Challenges

This is an asset that was originally for the TV show "See" on Paramount+. It's a scan of Jason Mamoa as the Baba Voss character. This asset was rushed, which meant it only needed rigging where it was required (the back and belly, mostly), but I thought it was a waste of a great character. I decided to rig it in my own pipeline, adding in a mouth and teeth so that I can rig a full face for this character. I was able to easily clean the model and migrate it into my own pipeline using scripts I've made and a maya plugin, Vetala. Some challenges I faced were due to the nature of how it originally came about, which would be rushed. One example was that there was no inner mouth geo, so I had to find a way to create the entire inside of the mouth and attach it to the original geometry, all while keeping the old skinning. I successfully did exactly that. Another challenge was creating a face rig, which is still a work in progress. I'll update this to include that once it's done! But, if you're curious to see how it's going, then check out my Youtube Lives

Lore

A fearless warrior: Baba Voss is the leader of the Alkenny Tribe, a skilled fighter, and a strong guardian. A family man: Baba Voss is the husband of Maghra and the adopted father of Kofun and Haniwa, twins with a dormant sense of sight. He prioritizes the safety of his family and tribe above all else. A secret: Baba Voss and Maghra keep the twins' sight a secret to protect them from the Witchfinders. They implore the twins to do the same for their own safety. A frequent advisor: Baba Voss seeks advice from Paris, who uses her wisdom to help him find ways to keep the tribe safe. A sacrifice: Baba sacrifices himself in a battle with Sibeth and Tormada by detonating a bomb that destroys them.
